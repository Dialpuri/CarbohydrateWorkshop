1. Introduction

Coot is a program for macromolecular model building, model completion and validation1. We will be using Coot alongside CCP4i22to build and refine a carbohydrate chain on a human glutamate carboxypeptidase II protein3 (PDB Code: 6FE5). This structure was solved up to 1.52 Å resolution with approximately 700 residues.
